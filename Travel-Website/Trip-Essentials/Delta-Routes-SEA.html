🔀Connection Strategy from SEA
🇪🇺 EUROPE — SEA nonstop first
AMS, CDG, LHR year-round. BCN & FCO summer 2026. For other European cities, connect via AMS (KLM) or CDG (Air France) on the nonstop SEA flight.
🌏 ASIA — SEA is your best hub
4 nonstop Asia routes: HND, ICN, PVG, TPE. For deeper Asia, connect via ICN→Korean Air. For HKG and Australia, connect via LAX.
🌎 LATIN AMERICA — Route through ATL
ATL has nonstops to BOG, CTG, LIM, SCL, GRU, GIG, GUA, PTY. JFK for Buenos Aires only.
🌍 AFRICA/MIDDLE EAST — Via ATL
ATL has nonstop to JNB, LOS, ACC, RAK, RUH (Oct 2026). Tel Aviv via JFK (resumes Sept 6, 2026). ATL is always faster than going via Europe.
🇵🇹 PORTUGAL — New in 2026
JFK–OPO daily (new 2026). SEA→JFK→OPO = 1 connection. JFK–LIS year-round.
⏱ MINIMIZE CONNECTIONS
SEA nonstops first. Then: AMS/CDG (Europe), ICN/HND (Asia), ATL (Latin America & Africa), JFK (transatlantic variety).
