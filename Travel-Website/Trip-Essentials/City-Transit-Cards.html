🇪🇺 Europe
🇬🇧 London Oyster Card tfl.gov.uk
Underground · Overground · Elizabeth line · DLR · Bus · Tram · some National Rail
Get it
Any Tube station ticket machine or visitor centre
Deposit
£5 refundable; load any amount on top
Tip
Contactless bank cards and Apple/Google Pay work on all TfL transport with the same daily and weekly fare caps — skip the Oyster entirely if your card is contactless.
🇫🇷 Paris Navigo Easy navigo.fr
Métro · RER · Bus · Tram · Transilien
Get it
Any Métro station ticket machine; €2 card fee
Options
Navigo Easy (PAYG/carnets) · Navigo Liberté+ (monthly PAYG) · Navigo Découverte (weekly/monthly flat)
Tip
Navigo Découverte (weekly pass, ~€30) covers all zones Mon–Sun and is cheaper than individual tickets for any stay over 4 days. Weeks run Monday–Sunday, not 7 rolling days.
🇳🇱 Amsterdam OV-chipkaart ov-chipkaart.nl
GVB Tram · Metro · Bus · NS trains · Intercity
Get it
GVB service points, station machines, or Schiphol airport
Deposit
€7.50 refundable; separate balance for GVB and NS
Tip
GVB trams and metro now accept contactless bank cards and Apple/Google Pay directly — no OV-chipkaart needed for city transit. You still need it for NS intercity trains.
🇩🇪 Berlin BVG Ticket / Berlin WelcomeCard bvg.de
U-Bahn · S-Bahn · Bus · Tram · Ferry
Get it
Station machines, BVG app, or Berlin WelcomeCard at airport and hotels
Options
Single (€3.50) · Day ticket (€10.40) · 7-day (€41.30) · WelcomeCard includes museum discounts
Tip
Berlin has no reloadable card — buy the BVG app on your phone for single trips or use a day/week paper ticket. Validate before boarding; inspectors check frequently.
🇮🇹 Rome BIT / Time-based passes atac.roma.it
Metro · Bus · Tram
Get it
Tabacchi, newsstands, Metro station machines, ATAC app
Options
BIT single €2 (100 min) · 24h €7 · 48h €12.50 · 72h €18 · 7-day €24
Tip
Validate every time you board — even with a multi-day pass. Inspectors are active on the Metro and buses near tourist areas. Machines often out of service; buy at tabacchi instead.
🇪🇸 Barcelona T-Casual tmb.cat
Metro · Bus · Tram · FGC · Rodalies (suburban rail)
Get it
Metro station machines; requires a reloadable T-Card or use physical T-Casual
Cost
T-Casual: 10 trips ~€12.15 (Zone 1) · shareable between travellers · single trip €2.55
Tip
One T-Casual card can be used by multiple people — tap once per person. The Hola BCN tourist pass (2–5 days unlimited) is worth it only if you make 4+ trips per day.
🇪🇸 Madrid Multi Card / Tourist Travel Pass metromadrid.es
Metro · EMT Bus · Cercanías (suburban rail)
Get it
Metro station machines; Tourist Travel Pass at airport T4 or Nuevos Ministerios
Options
Single €1.50–€2 (zone-based) · Tourist Pass Zone A: 1-day €8.40, 2-day €14.20, 3-day €18.40, 5-day €26.80, 7-day €35.40
Tip
The Tourist Travel Pass includes airport Line 8 surcharge (€3 normally). Zone A covers all central Madrid attractions. Get it at the airport on arrival — it pays off immediately.
🇦🇹 Vienna Wiener Linien Pass wienerlinien.at
U-Bahn · Tram · Bus · S-Bahn (within Vienna zones)
Get it
U-Bahn station machines, Tabak shops, WienMobil app
Options
24h €8 · 48h €14.10 · 72h €17.10 · 8-day €40.80 (non-consecutive days OK) · Vienna City Card adds museum discounts
Tip
The 8-day pass is 8 individual day validations — stamp once per day, usable across multiple trips. Ideal for longer stays. Validate at the blue stamping machines on first use each day.
🇵🇹 Lisbon Viva Viagem metrolisboa.pt
Metro · Carris Bus · Tram · Ferry · Suburban trains (CP)
Get it
Any Metro station machine; €0.50 card fee
Options
Zapping (PAYG credit) · 24h unlimited €6.80 · Lisboa Card includes museums and transport
Tip
Load Zapping credit — it works on Metro, bus, tram, and Tagus ferries at the lowest per-trip rate. Historic tram lines 28 and 15E accept the card; avoid buying single tickets on board.
🇨🇿 Prague Lítačka / Time tickets dpp.cz
Metro · Tram · Bus · Funicular
Get it
Metro machines, DPP offices, tobacconists, PID Lítačka app, or SMS
Options
30 min CZK 30 · 90 min CZK 40 · 24h CZK 120 · 3-day CZK 330 · short-term paper or app-based
Tip
Buy tickets before boarding — on-board purchase only available on some night buses at a surcharge. Validate on entry each time. Night trams run on separate routes 00:30–04:30.
🌏 Asia
🇯🇵 Tokyo Suica / Pasmo jreast.co.jp
JR lines · Tokyo Metro · Toei Subway · Bus · Monorail · convenience stores · taxis
Get it
JR station machines, or add Suica to Apple Wallet (iPhone with Japanese region or via Express Transit) — no physical card needed
Deposit
¥500 refundable for physical card; none for Apple Wallet
Tip
Suica works at nearly every transit system across Japan — not just Tokyo. Also accepted at 7-Eleven, FamilyMart, Lawson, and most vending machines. Add it to Apple Wallet before departure for the simplest setup.
🇯🇵 Osaka / Kyoto ICOCA westjr.co.jp
JR West · Osaka Metro · Keihan · Hankyu · Kintetsu · most local transit
Get it
JR West ticket machines at Osaka, Kyoto, or Kansai Airport stations
Deposit
¥500 refundable; load as needed at any JR machine
Tip
ICOCA is fully interoperable with Suica — if you already have a Suica from Tokyo, use it in Osaka and Kyoto without getting ICOCA. Both work at convenience stores throughout Japan.
🇭🇰 Hong Kong Octopus Card octopus.com.hk
MTR · Bus · Tram · Ferry · Light Rail · Airport Express · 7-Eleven · convenience stores
Get it
Airport MTR station or any MTR customer service centre; also available as app
Deposit
HK$50 refundable; minimum HK$100 initial load
Tip
Refund the full balance at any MTR station on departure — staff can process it even with a negative balance. The Octopus app (HK residents) replaces the physical card but requires a local bank account.
🇸🇬 Singapore EZ-Link / Contactless ezlink.com.sg
MRT · LRT · Bus
Get it
TransitLink counters at MRT stations, 7-Eleven; S$5 non-refundable card fee
Contactless
Visa/Mastercard contactless and Apple/Google Pay accepted on all MRT and bus — no EZ-Link needed
Tip
Skip EZ-Link entirely — just tap your contactless card or phone. Fares are identical and you avoid the S$5 non-refundable card fee. Daily and weekly caps apply to contactless too.
🇰🇷 Seoul T-money t-money.co.kr
Metro · Bus · some taxis · convenience stores
Get it
CU, GS25, 7-Eleven, or Ministop convenience stores; W3,000–4,000 card fee
Transfers
Free transfer between metro and bus within 30 minutes — tap on and off every time
Tip
Always tap off when exiting the bus and metro — distance-based fares mean not tapping off overcharges you. Reload at convenience stores or metro machines. T-money also works in Busan, Daegu, and most Korean cities.
🇹🇼 Taipei EasyCard easycard.com.tw
MRT · Bus · Taiwan Railways · YouBike · convenience stores
Get it
Any MRT station service centre or 7-Eleven; NT$100 refundable deposit
Discount
20% discount on MRT fares vs single-trip tickets
Tip
Refund remaining balance at any MRT station on departure. The EasyCard also works on YouBike city bikes at a discounted rate — first 30 min free for MRT-connected trips.
🌎 Americas
🇺🇸 New York OMNY (tap to pay) omny.mta.info
Subway · Local Bus · Express Bus · Staten Island Railway
Get it
No card needed — tap any contactless Visa/Mastercard, or Apple/Google Pay at any turnstile
Fare cap
$34/week cap — after 12 paid rides in a week, additional rides are free through Sunday
Tip
MetroCard is being phased out. OMNY contactless is the current standard — just tap your phone or card. The OMNY card (available at station machines) works identically if you prefer a dedicated card.
🇺🇸 Washington DC SmarTrip wmata.com
Metro · Metrobus · DC Circulator · MARC · VRE
Get it
Metro station machines, CVS/Walgreens, or add to Apple/Google Wallet; $2 card fee
Fares
Distance-based $2.25–$6.75 peak / $2–$3.85 off-peak; bus $2 flat
Tip
Always tap out when exiting the Metro — distance-based fares require exit tap. Forgetting to tap out charges the maximum fare. Contactless bank cards now accepted at most Metro gates.
🇺🇸 Chicago Ventra ventrachicago.com
CTA El (train) · CTA Bus
Get it
CTA station machines, Walgreens, or Ventra app; $5 card fee (refunded after $5 in rides)
Options
PAYG $2.50/ride with free transfer within 2h · 1-day $10 · 3-day $20 · 7-day $28
Tip
The Ventra app works as a mobile card — load it and tap your phone at turnstiles. Contactless bank cards accepted at all CTA gates. Free transfer applies once within a 2-hour window (train-to-bus or bus-to-bus).
🇦🇺 Oceania
🇦🇺 Sydney Opal Card opal.com.au
Train · Bus · Ferry · Light Rail
Get it
Convenience stores, newsagencies, station machines, or opal.com.au; no card fee
Fare cap
Daily cap A$17.80 · Weekly cap A$50 (free travel once cap reached)
Tip
Contactless bank cards and Apple/Google Pay accepted on all Opal-enabled transport — same fares and caps as the Opal card. No separate card needed if you tap with your phone or contactless card.
🇦🇺 Melbourne Myki ptv.vic.gov.au
Train · Tram · Bus
Get it
7-Eleven, train stations, Melbourne Airport; A$6 non-refundable card fee
Free tram zone
CBD tram network is free — no need to tap within the Free Tram Zone boundary
Tip
Always touch on and off for trains and buses — failure to touch off is charged the maximum fare. Trams only require touch on (not off). The A$6 card fee is not refundable; buy one on arrival and top up as needed.
