Travel Guides

Updated June 2026
📕
Fodor's
Editorial Guide
Curated city guides, hotel and restaurant picks — strong editorial standard. Good starting point for building a shortlist of neighborhoods, museums, and dining.
🎨
Culture Trip
Editorial Guide
Culturally-driven destination guides with a focus on arts, food, and local perspective. Useful for finding what a city is actually about beyond the headline attractions.
📗
Rough Guides
Editorial Guide
In-depth destination coverage across history, culture, and practical detail. One of the most thorough written guides for understanding a place before you arrive.
🔭
Atlas Obscura
Hidden Places
Hidden and unusual places around the world — the stops no mainstream guidebook covers. Excellent for finding something genuinely different in a city you've already been to.
🇪🇺
Rick Steves
Europe
Practical Europe travel — neighborhood-level breakdowns, free audio guides, and crowd-management tips. Best for first-time visits to major European cities.
🌍
National Geographic
Inspiration
Destination inspiration, best-of lists, and photography-driven guides. More useful for deciding where to go than for planning once you have a destination.
📖
Wikipedia
Reference
Primary reference for stop history, context, and factual detail. Every stop's Wikipedia article is the first read before building any itinerary entry.
⭐
TripAdvisor
Reviews
Attraction rankings, traveler reviews, and things to do by category. Best used for cross-referencing ratings rather than as a primary discovery source.
