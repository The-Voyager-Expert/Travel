Entry Requirements

Pre-entry authorizations and e-visas for US passport holders. Countries requiring a full visa application are on the Visas page.

ETIAS is launching Q4 2026. Americans visiting France, Italy, Spain, Germany, and 27 other Schengen countries will need a quick online pre-authorization (€20, valid 3 years) — similar to the US ESTA. Mandatory enforcement begins around April 2027 after a grace period. No action needed today, but worth knowing before you book a 2027 trip.

No Pre-Authorization Needed

US passport holders enter these countries visa-free with no pre-travel step. Standard rules apply: passport valid for at least 6 months beyond arrival, and no overstaying.

Asia — Pacific
Japan · South Korea · Taiwan · Singapore · Thailand · Vietnam · Hong Kong · Bali (Indonesia) · Palawan (Philippines) · Sri Lanka (visa-free since Sep 2024)
Europe (non-Schengen)
Ireland (Dublin) · Georgia (Tbilisi · 365 days) · Albania
Middle East
UAE (Dubai · Abu Dhabi) · Qatar (Doha)
Latin America
Mexico · Costa Rica · Argentina · Peru · Chile · Colombia · Uruguay
Caribbean
Puerto Rico · US Virgin Islands (US territory) · Aruba · Barbados · Cayman Islands · Turks & Caicos · Sint Maarten · Bahamas · Curaçao
North America
Canada · Mexico

Entry rules change. Verify requirements at travel.state.gov before every trip — especially for ETIAS, which is rolling out in late 2026.
