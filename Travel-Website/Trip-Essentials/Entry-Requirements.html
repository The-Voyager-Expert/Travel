Pre-entry authorizations and e-visas for US passport holders. Countries requiring a full visa application are on the Visas page.

ETIAS has been delayed. In July 2026, EU-Lisa confirmed that a Q4 2026 launch was no longer feasible after technical issues with the EES border system. A revised timeline is expected in late 2026. Americans will eventually need a quick online pre-authorization (€20, valid 3 years) to visit France, Italy, Spain, Germany, and 27 other Schengen countries — similar to the US ESTA. No action needed until a launch date is confirmed.
