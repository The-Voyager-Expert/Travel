About These Programs
🛂 Global Entry
What Expedited US Customs clearance when returning from international trips. Includes TSA PreCheck.
Where After landing, follow signs to US Customs / Federal Inspection — usually the arrivals level. Look for the Global Entry kiosk bank before the CBP officer booths. Scan passport, fingerprints, done.
Enroll Apply online at ttp.cbp.dhs.gov, then schedule an interview at a CBP enrollment center — or use Enrollment on Arrival (EoA) after your next international flight at a participating airport.
cbp.gov/global-entry
🔵 CLEAR
What Biometric identity check at TSA security — replaces showing your ID. Doesn't skip the X-ray belt.
Where At the entrance to TSA security lanes — look for the blue CLEAR podium/kiosk near the ID-check area. A CLEAR ambassador scans your eyes or fingerprints, then walks you to the front of the PreCheck or regular X-ray lane.
Enroll Sign up online or walk up to any CLEAR location. Biometrics are captured on-site in under 5 minutes — no appointment needed.
clearme.com
