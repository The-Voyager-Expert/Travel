SkyTeam Lounges — Delta One ticket, SkyTeam Elite Plus, Flying Blue Gold+, or Sky Club membership (where accepted)
Priority Pass — included with Chase Sapphire Reserve (still 2 free guests) and other premium cards; Capital One Venture X now covers the primary cardholder only (Feb 2026) — guests are $35/visit, authorized users pay $125/yr for their own access
