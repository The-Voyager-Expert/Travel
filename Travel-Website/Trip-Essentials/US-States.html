Crime level note

Crime levels are relative classifications based on FBI Uniform Crime Reporting data (violent crime per 100K residents). Low = below 200 per 100K. Medium = 200–400 per 100K. High = above 400 per 100K. Rates vary significantly within a state — urban areas may differ substantially from statewide figures. Always check local advisories for your specific destination.
