Travel Apps

Apps worth having on your phone before every trip — organized by what they do.

Download offline content before you leave home. Google Maps areas, Google Translate language packs, and XE Currency rates all work without a data connection once downloaded. Do this on Wi-Fi — not in the airport lounge at departure.
Maps & Navigation
Google Maps
iOS · Android
The standard — works everywhere
Walking, driving, transit, and cycling directions worldwide. Download a city or region for offline use before departure. Street View is useful for orienting yourself before you arrive somewhere new.
FreeOffline mapsStreet View
Maps.me
iOS · Android
Fully offline, built on OpenStreetMap
Offline maps covering 200+ countries, useful where Google Maps is blocked or unreliable. Still downloadable and updated, but quality and community trust have declined since it dropped open-source development. Organic Maps is now the more recommended alternative among OpenStreetMap users.
FreeFull offlineHiking trails
Rome2rio
iOS · Android · Web
Multi-modal route planner
Enter any two places on Earth and it shows every way to get there — flights, trains, buses, ferries, rideshare — with costs and travel times. Useful for planning regional legs of a trip where you're not sure what exists.
FreeTrains + busesBooking links
Citymapper
iOS · Android
Best for urban transit in major cities
Detailed transit directions for 100+ cities — subway, bus, bike share, and scooter. More accurate than Google Maps for real-time transit in London, Paris, Tokyo, New York. Offline mode for covered cities.
FreeReal-time transitOffline mode
Flights
Flighty
iOS
Fastest flight alerts available
Sends delay and gate change notifications before the airline does — often 30+ minutes earlier. Delay prediction tells you if a flight is likely to be delayed based on incoming aircraft. Tracks flight history and stats over time.
Free / $4.99/mo ProDelay prediction
TripIt
iOS · Android
Itinerary organizer from email confirmations
Forward any booking confirmation email and TripIt builds a unified trip itinerary automatically — flights, hotels, rental cars, restaurant reservations. Pro version adds real-time alerts and seat tracking. Works offline.
Free / $49/yr ProAuto-parse email
Translation
Google Translate
iOS · Android
Point your camera at signs and menus
130+ languages, including instant camera translation that overlays translated text on-screen in real time. Download language packs for fully offline use — accuracy offline is nearly as good as online for most languages.
FreeCamera translateOffline packs
DeepL
iOS · Android · Web
More natural for European languages
Generally better than Google Translate for nuanced writing in French, German, Spanish, Italian, Polish, and other European languages. No camera mode — text and document translation only.
Free / ProDocument translate
Money & Currency
XE Currency
iOS · Android
Offline currency converter
Download exchange rates before you leave. Works fully offline for quick conversions at markets, restaurants, or when negotiating. Tracks multiple currencies at once. No account required.
FreeOffline ratesMulti-currency
Wise
iOS · Android
Spend and send at the real exchange rate
Debit card and account that converts at the mid-market rate with minimal fees. Hold and convert 50+ currencies. Withdraw from ATMs worldwide. Better than most bank cards for international spending. See also: Cards & ATM.
Free accountReal rate50+ currencies
Safety & Alerts
Smart Traveler (STEP)
iOS · Android
US State Department enrollment + alerts
Enroll your trips with the Smart Traveler Enrollment Program so the nearest US Embassy can contact you in an emergency. Sends country-specific security alerts, entry requirement updates, and health notices.
FreeEmbassy alertsTrip enrollment
Delta / Airline App
iOS · Android
Boarding passes + same-day rebooking
Download boarding passes before you need them. Delta's app lets you rebook directly during irregular operations without waiting on hold. Add your flights to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet for offline access at the gate.
FreeOffline boarding pass
eSIM & Connectivity
Airalo
iOS · Android
eSIM marketplace — buy before you land
Buy and install a local eSIM for 200+ countries before you leave — no physical SIM swap. Cheaper than carrier international plans for data-heavy trips. Full comparison of eSIM options on the Phone & SIM Abroad page.
Free appPay per plan200+ countries
WhatsApp
iOS · Android
International messaging and calls over Wi-Fi
The default messaging app in most of Europe, Latin America, and Asia. Free calls and messages over Wi-Fi or data. Useful for contacting hotels, restaurants, and tour operators who don't answer email quickly.
FreeVoice + video calls
Planning & Packing
PackPoint
iOS · Android
Smart packing list generator
Enter your destination, trip dates, and activities. PackPoint generates a packing list adjusted for the weather forecast and what you're doing — beach day vs. business dinner vs. hiking. Catches things you'd forget.
Free / $2.99 Premium
Google Lens
iOS · Android (built into Google app)
Point-and-search for menus, signs, and places
Translate menus in real time, identify landmarks, search for things visually, scan QR codes. Built into Google Photos and the Google app — no separate download needed on Android; install the Google app on iOS.
