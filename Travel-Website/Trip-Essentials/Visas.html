🇱🇦 LaoseVisa Luang Prabang Apply at evisa.laopdr.gov.la — 30-day single-entry tourist eVisa, ~USD 50, typically approved within 3 business days. Visa on arrival (~USD 35) also available at Luang Prabang International Airport.
🇻🇳 VietnameVisa Hanoi · Hoi An Apply at evisa.gov.vn — 90-day single-entry ~US$25 or multiple-entry ~US$50, processed in 3–5 business days. Visa on arrival also available at international airports with a pre-approved letter. US citizens are not covered by Vietnam's 45-day visa exemption and need this eVisa for mainland travel. The one carve-out: Phú Quốc island allows a 30-day visa-free stay if you arrive there directly from abroad.

Cash or card at the airport counter before immigration, or apply for the e-VoA online beforehand. Bali arrivals also pay a separate Bali Tourist Levy (IDR 150,000, ~US$10).
🇲🇻 Maldives30 days free Maldives US citizens receive a free 30-day tourist visa on arrival at Velana International Airport (MLE). Passport valid 6+ months, confirmed accommodation and onward ticket required.
🇯🇴 Jordan40 JOD (~$56) Petra US citizens receive a single-entry visa on arrival at Queen Alia International Airport (AMM) and most border crossings. Valid for 30 days. Consider the Jordan Pass — if you plan to visit Petra, the pass covers the visa fee and Petra entry ticket when purchased before arrival and staying a minimum of 3 nights.
