🟢 Europe — Schengen Area · visa-free 21 countries
No visa. Stay up to 90 days within any 180-day period — and that 90 days is a shared pool across all Schengen countries combined, not per country.
Heads-up — ETIAS, late 2026: the EU's ETIAS travel authorization (a quick paid online form, ~€7, like the US ESTA) is expected to launch in the last quarter of 2026. It is not required yet — for now you travel passport-only. Once live, you'll file it before each trip.
