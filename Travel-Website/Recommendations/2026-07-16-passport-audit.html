Passport Page — Annual Audit

Run: 2026-07-16 · passport-annual-audit routine · Source: travel.state.gov
Overall: The page was in good shape for processing times, method cards, and tips — all data confirmed accurate against current travel.state.gov content. Four content gaps were found and fixed: minor-passport fees (book/card/combo) were missing entirely, the in-person execution/acceptance fee was undocumented, the "Book + card together" savings note was factually wrong ($30 when actual savings is $35 in execution fees, in-person only), and a new State Dept policy banning AI-edited passport photos was not covered. All four are now corrected on the live page. Three UX/CSS issues (dead step-card CSS, inline styles) are logged in the dated hand-off file for the UX routine.

Fixed

  • "Book + card together" savings note corrected — was "Save $30 vs. buying separately" (incorrect: there is no discount on application fees; the only savings is $35 on the acceptance fee for in-person applicants). Now reads: "Applying for both together saves $35 in acceptance fees vs. two separate in-person visits."

Added

  • Minor passport book — $100 application fee · Under age 16 · 5-year validity (row was absent from the Costs table)
  • Minor passport card — $15 application fee · Under age 16 · land & sea only (row was absent)
  • Minor book + card together — $115 combined application fee · Under age 16 (row was absent)
  • Execution / acceptance fee — $35 · in-person applicants only · paid directly to acceptance facility · not required for mail-in or online renewal (fee type was entirely absent from the page)
  • AI-edited passport photos tip — State Dept now actively screens all submissions and rejects AI-generated or digitally altered images; tip added to "Key Things to Know" section
