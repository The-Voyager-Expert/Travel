Root cause fixed: commons_photo.py --download now (a) applies ImageOps.exif_transpose() on every download, (b) resizes by longest-side not always width=800, and (c) hard-blocks the save when the downloaded orientation doesn't match the Commons original — forcing the builder to find a better source image.
