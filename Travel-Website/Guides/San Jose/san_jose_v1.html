🏨 From Hotel: 🚕 15 min → Winchester Mystery House
1. Winchester Mystery House
↳ A 160-room Victorian mansion built from 1884 to 1922 with no master plan. California Historical Landmark and National Register property. Known for stairs that rise only 10 feet across 44 steps, doors opening onto sheer drops, and Tiffany spider-web windows.
📖 Wikipedia

🚕 10 min → Municipal Rose Garden
2. San Jose Municipal Rose Garden
↳ Covers 5.5 acres with over 3,500 rose shrubs across 189 varieties. Designated America's Best Rose Garden by All-America Rose Selections in 2010 and an official AARS test site receiving award-winning cultivars before public release. Established 1927; free admission year-round.
📖 Wikipedia
🏛️ Daily 8:00am - 8:00pm
⏰ ~1 hr
🆓 Free

🚶 8 min · 🚕 4 min → Rosicrucian Egyptian Museum
3. Rosicrucian Egyptian Museum
↳ Holds the largest collection of ancient Egyptian antiquities in the Western United States — more than 2,000 objects including mummies, canopic jars, and funerary papyri. Buildings are the only museum structures in the world in Ancient Egyptian style. Full-scale replica rock-cut tomb based on the Beni Hasan tombs.
📖 Wikipedia
🏛️ Friday 10:00am - 5:00pm · Saturday - Sunday 11:00am - 6:00pm
🚫 Closed Monday - Thursday
⏰ ~1 hr 30 min
📍 1660 Park Ave · Rose Garden
🚕 15 min → The Tech Interactive
4. The Tech Interactive
↳ Silicon Valley's public science center in a 1998 Ricardo Legorreta building. Covers biotech, robotics, clean energy, and computing through interactive exhibits including a BioTinkering Lab, robotics challenge floors, and an IMAX Dome theater — one of the largest domed screens in the western United States.
📖 Wikipedia

🍮 Local Tastes
Pho
↳ San Jose has the largest Vietnamese diaspora community outside Vietnam. Pho along Story Road and King Road in East San Jose is regionally diverse and among the most authentic in the country.
📍 Little Saigon · Story Rd · East Side
Com Tam
↳ Vietnamese broken rice served with grilled pork chop, fried egg, and pickled daikon. San Jose's version from the Grand Century Mall food court is considered definitive outside Vietnam.
📍 Grand Century Mall Food Court · 111 Story Rd · East Side
Street Tacos
↳ San Jose's large Mexican-American community runs some of the best taquerias in California. Alum Rock Ave is the center of the city's al pastor, carnitas, and birria tradition.
📍 Alum Rock Ave · East Side
