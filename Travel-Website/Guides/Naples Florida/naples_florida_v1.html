1. Corkscrew Swamp Sanctuary
↳ National Audubon Society preserve spanning 13,000 acres with North America's largest remaining virgin bald cypress forest. Elevated 2.25-mile boardwalk winds through cypress swamp, pine flatwoods, wet prairie. Regular sightings: alligators, roseate spoonbills, wood storks, osprey.
📖 Wikipedia

🍮 Local Tastes
Stone Crab
↳ Naples' signature seasonal delicacy (October 15 - May 1). Served hot with drawn butter or chilled with tangy mustard sauce. Truluck's, Kelly's Fish House, and dedicated seafood counters throughout downtown.
Grouper Sandwich
↳ Local coastal classic of fried grouper from local fishermen, served with fries and coleslaw in a bun. Grouper & Chips is the must-visit destination for this iconic sandwich.
Sanibel Pink Shrimp
↳ Regional delicacy found on many menus. Fresh Gulf shrimp with distinctive pink color and sweet flavor profile.
Gulf Seafood
↳ Fresh-catch preparations of mahi-mahi, snapper, local fish. Most restaurants feature daily changing seafood specials from that morning's catch.

🚌 Getting Around
Rideshare
Uber and Lyft widely available throughout Naples. Typical ride cost within city limits: $8-$18.
Public Transit
Collier Area Transit (CAT) bus system. Limited coverage. Ride passes available via rideCAT app.
Walking
Downtown Naples (5th Avenue South, 3rd Street South) is pedestrian-friendly for shopping, galleries, and dining. Most other attractions require car or rideshare.
Biking
30+ miles of bike lanes and shared-use paths. Flat terrain ideal for cycling. Many destinations accessible by bike from downtown.
Car Rental
Recommended for exploring beyond downtown. Distances between attractions make car rental beneficial for a 3-day stay.
❗️ Heads Up
Stone Crab Season
Available October 15 - May 1 only. Summer and early fall travel will not include this signature delicacy on menus.
Naples Botanical Garden Closure
Closed October - May for maintenance. Plan this attraction only if traveling June-September.
Museum Hours
Baker Museum closes Monday and has afternoon-only hours on Sunday. Verify hours before visiting.
Hurricane Season
Naples is in the Gulf Coast hurricane belt (June-November). Summer travel carries higher storm risk. Tours and outdoor activities may be cancelled on short notice.
