↳ A pristine alpine lake in Many Glacier valley surrounded by 3,000-foot peaks and hanging glaciers. Turquoise water reflects Mount Cleveland and Mount Jackson. Historic boathouse offers boat shuttles reducing hiking distances.

🚌 Getting Around
🚕 Ride Apps
Uber and Lyft have limited availability in East Glacier. Reliable car rental from Kalispell airport is recommended.
🚌 Shuttle Services
Seasonal shuttle services run along Going-to-the-Sun Road and Many Glacier area during July–early September. Historic Xanterra shuttle buses connect key trailheads and reduce driving stress on narrow mountain sections.
🚙 Car Rental
Enterprise, Hertz, and Budget have desks at Kalispell Regional Airport, 45 minutes from East Glacier. A personal vehicle is essential for accessing park roads and trailheads.
🚢 Boat Shuttles
Glacier Park Boat Company operates daily boat shuttles on Swiftcurrent Lake, Many Glacier valley, from 7am–5pm during seasonal months. Shuttles reduce hiking distances by 2–4 miles on backcountry trail routes.
🚆 Train Stations
East Glacier Park Station
Amtrak Empire Builder stops here; historic 1913 depot is 0.5 mile from East Glacier Park Lodge. Daily service connects Seattle, Glacier, and Chicago on a scenic alpine routing.
📖 Wikipedia
📍 Station Street · East Glacier Park

⚠️ Heads Up
Weather is highly variable in the mountains; bring layers even in summer. Going-to-the-Sun Road (primary route) is seasonal and often closed October–May. Book accommodations early in peak season (July–August).
