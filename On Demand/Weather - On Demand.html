🌤 Weather

Status: ON DEMAND — researched only when asked. Findings surface in chat; the guide never includes a Weather section.

Summary: Trusted forecast sources for live weather checks closer to a trip — Weather.com, AccuWeather, Ventusky, Windy.com. Forecasts shift too much for the guide to carry static weather notes.

Sources

  • Weather.com — 10–14 day forecast · best for rain probability and long-range trends
  • AccuWeather — best for precise hourly timing
  • Ventusky — best for cloud cover and visibility · essential before any scenic day or viewpoint
  • Windy.com — second model · best as cross-check when forecasts disagree
