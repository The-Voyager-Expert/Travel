Booking rules

  • Origin — Seattle, WA (SEA). Round-trip by default.
  • Airline — Delta and SkyTeam partners only. Non-Delta/non-SkyTeam carriers are not searched, suggested, or compared — even when cheaper.
  • Class — First Class preferred · coach acceptable. Delta One on international only.
  • Loyalty status — Dani and Leo are Delta Platinum Medallion. On domestic, book Comfort+ as bridge fare; Platinum upgrades clear into First automatically.
  • Routing — direct preferred, fewest connections.
  • Lounge — check whether the departure airport has a Delta One lounge. Sky Club access is automatic.
  • Booking site — book on delta.com. Price-check on Google Flights first.
  • Refundability — non-refundable only. eCredit on cancellation gets used on the next trip.
