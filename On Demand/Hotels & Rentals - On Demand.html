🏨 Hotels & Rentals

Status: ON DEMAND — researched only when asked. Output goes to Trips.html; the guide never auto-includes booking research.

Summary: How accommodations get picked for the trip — hotels by default (they appear on the guide's title card), rentals as the fallback when no qualifying hotel exists in the area.

Anchor

Hotel selection anchors on the conference venue address. For non-conference trips, anchor on whatever is specified at the time of the request.

Experience-calibrated, not aspirational

Requirements reflect what has repeatedly been achievable on real trips, not what would be preferred in the abstract. Tightening to aim higher is not allowed; loosening for flexibility is not allowed. Edits to any threshold require explicit direction.

Label ranking:

  • required — blocks the pick. Without it, move on.
  • strongly preferred — compromise only when nothing else qualifies.
  • preferred — nice to have when present, not a blocker.

Hotels

Requirements

Apply to both US and International unless noted.

  • Rating — 9.0+ on Booking.com · 4.5+ elsewhere
  • Location:
    • US — max 30 min by car from the conference venue (few US cities walk)
    • International — max 30 min walking from the conference venue
  • Modern — no rustic or outdated decor
  • 24h reception
  • AC — required US · preferred International
  • Bed setup — required: 1 king OR 2 beds. A single queen / double / full alone is not acceptable.
  • Free cancellation — strongly preferred
  • Noise — note if noisy
  • Parking — required when renting a car
  • Preferred extras — large windows · spacious rooms · breakfast on-site · balcony or views

Brand priority — US

Marriott Residence Inn first — compromise on luxury for amazing space.

If unavailable: best fit among the Marriott · Hilton · Hyatt families, no particular order. All sub-brands count.

Sub-brand reference:

  • Marriott family — Marriott, JW Marriott, Ritz-Carlton, Sheraton, Westin, Renaissance, Courtyard, AC Hotels, Aloft, Autograph Collection.
  • Hilton family — Hilton, DoubleTree, Hampton Inn, Embassy Suites, Conrad, Waldorf Astoria, Hilton Garden Inn, Curio Collection.
  • Hyatt family — Hyatt, Hyatt Place, Hyatt Regency, Andaz, Grand Hyatt, Park Hyatt, Thompson Hotels.

  • Marriott family — Marriott, JW Marriott, Ritz-Carlton, Sheraton, Westin, Renaissance, Courtyard, AC Hotels, Aloft, Autograph Collection

  • Hyatt family — Hyatt, Hyatt Place, Hyatt Regency, Andaz, Grand Hyatt, Park Hyatt, Thompson Hotels

Second tier — consider only when no first-tier brand qualifies:

  • Radisson Blu

Brands outside this list do not count — including budget tiers like Ibis, Mercure, Park Inn, Holiday Inn (even though they sit in the same families as Sofitel, Pullman, Radisson Blu, Intercontinental respectively).

Where to book

Decision flow:

  • US + preferred brand (Marriott / Hilton / Hyatt families) → book direct on the chain site.
  • US + anything else → aggregator (Booking.com → Expedia → TripAdvisor).
  • International + preferred brand → check both brand site AND aggregators; surface the comparison.
  • Target hotel sold out on the channel above → walk every other platform before pivoting to a different property. This overrides the three rows above.

1. US — preferred brands: direct only

Book directly on the chain website (marriott.com, hilton.com, hyatt.com).

2. US — anything else: aggregator

Book via Booking.com → Expedia → TripAdvisor (same search order).

3. International — preferred brands: check both

Check both the brand's own website and the aggregators (Booking.com → Expedia → TripAdvisor) — channels diverge on price and cancellation terms.
Surface the comparison; do not auto-pick.

4. Aggregator fallback — get the hotel, not the channel

Trigger: the target hotel is identified, and the channel from rules 1–3 shows sold out.

Action: walk every other platform before pivoting to a different property:

  • US preferred brand, chain site sold out → Booking.com → Expedia → TripAdvisor (overrides rule 1).
  • International preferred brand, one platform sold out → check the others.
  • Anywhere, one aggregator sold out → check the next aggregator before giving up.

Pivot to a different property only when every platform shows sold out.

After booking via aggregator (US case): optionally retry the chain site later. If the chain site has availability and the aggregator booking has free cancellation, switch to direct and cancel the aggregator booking.

Rentals

Requirements

  • Entire home only — house, townhouse, condo, apartment. No rooms.
  • Rating — 4.5+
  • Location — max 30 min by car from the conference venue (both US and International). Rentals only happen when a qualifying hotel can't be found — typically harder-to-reach locations where walking isn't the mode anyway.
  • AC — required US · preferred International
  • Washer & dryer — required US · preferred International
  • 2 beds (not bedrooms) — strongly preferred
  • Kitchen — preferred
  • Modern — preferred
  • Parking — required when renting a car
