## 📋 Dani's Non-Negotiables

- **Fully enclosed bathroom**: sink, shower, AND toilet all behind a closed door. Any open-to-bedroom design = instant reject.
- **Free cancel always**: non-refundable only if literally no other option exists.
- **Bed**: king or double. Queen is not comfortable for 2.
- **Vibe**: modern, clean, spacious. No boutique quirks, no "charming historic" small rooms.
- **Location**: quiet, local neighborhood strongly preferred. Centraal Station area and Dam Square = too touristy/noisy. De Pijp, de Plantage, Jordaan-type areas are ideal. Canals nearby is a plus but not mandatory.
