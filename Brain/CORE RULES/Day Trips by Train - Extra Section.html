⛲️ Day Trips by Train

Phase: 5 — Per-section build

Summary: Nearby cities worth a day trip, reachable by train from the guide city.

§ 1. What ships

Search day trips from [City] across reputable travel guides — Fodor's, Culture Trip, Rough Guides, Rick Steves, National Geographic, and official or local [City] tourism websites — and add every destination they recommend. These sources already filter for quality and define what counts as a day trip, so trust their picks.

§ 2. What does not ship

2.1. A [City] Train Day itinerary stop never repeats here.

2.2. A guide whose [City] is an island ships no entries.

§ 3. Minimum entries — European-country guides

A guide whose city is in a European country ships at least five entries in this section.

§ 4. Entry Format(s)

The destination city is always shown (day trips are always to a city different from the guide city). The country is included only when the destination is in a different country from the guide city [City]. No postal code.

🎫 book at: [Train Ticket Operator] or Omio

Omio coverage exception. Omio does not cover all regions. When the guide city is in a region where Omio has no service (mainland China), use book at: [Train Ticket Operator] without the or Omio suffix. Where Omio covers the region, or Omio is always the second entry in the book at: row, written exactly as Omio in title case. The validator omio-required check is scoped to Omio-covered regions only.

§ 5. When none qualify

Ship the negative-finding line:

No day trips from [City].
