🍮 Local Tastes

Phase: 5 — Per-section build

Summary: Foods or drinks famous in or typical of the destination — locally invented, tied to a specific café or regional tradition, authentic to this place.

§ 1. What ships

Include only when something genuinely distinctive exists — tied to the place's identity. Examples: French onion soup, bicerin, currywurst, Pierre Hermé and Ladurée macarons.

§ 2. Entry Format(s)

[Dish or Drink Name] Title Case (not all caps)
↳ [Description] ≤240 chars: what it is, origin, what makes it distinctive
📍 [Establishment] · [Establishment] · [Establishment] each establishment name goes to its location; use as many as are relevant

§ 3. When none qualify
