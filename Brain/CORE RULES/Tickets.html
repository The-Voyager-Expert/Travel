🎟 Tickets

Phase: 4 — Per-stop build

Summary: Booking source and entry format for a ticket box — the skip-the-line ticket option for a Self-Guided Stop.

§ 1. Booking source

Choose the booking platform based on stop type. Venue site is always the last resort.

US live events and performances (concerts, theater, sports): Ticketmaster, StubHub, or TodayTix. Venue site as fallback.

US attractions, museums, and venues: Viator, GetYourGuide, or TripAdvisor. Venue site as fallback.

International: Viator, GetYourGuide, or TripAdvisor. Any popular local platform (e.g. Klook in Asia) or venue site as fallback.

§ 2. Entry format

Three shapes — the source determines which applies.

No rating or review floor. Unlike Tours, a ticket box has no minimum rating or review count. The attraction is already chosen for the itinerary; the box only supplies the booking URL, so a ticket ships whatever its rating — or with no rating at all (shape 2c). A low rating never blocks a ticket.

2a. Via platform

(Viator · GetYourGuide · TripAdvisor · Klook) — review count required:

🎟 [Ticket Title] · [N.N]⭐ · [Review Count]+ reviews · [Booking Platform]

2b. Venue site

(Last resort) — no review count required:

🎟 [Ticket Title] · [N.N]⭐ · [venue.domain]

2c. URL-only

(When no title or rating is available):

🎟 [Booking Platform]
