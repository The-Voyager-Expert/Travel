🍽️ Downtown Restaurants

Phase: 5 — Per-section build

Summary: The 5 best restaurants in the destination's historic downtown core — the named central district that defines the city for visitors. The kind of place worth crossing the city for.

§ 1. Reference section

Downtown Restaurants is a reference section, not an itinerary stop. Address row only — no walk or ride rows, since the section is district-scoped, not hotel-scoped.

§ 2. What defines "downtown"

Historic downtown: the named historic district the destination is most identified with — the centro histórico, ciutat vella, altstadt, downtown core. When in doubt, the area inside the medieval walls or the area Wikipedia describes as the "historic centre".

Metropolis downtown: the central business district — the dense urban core of office towers, major transit hubs, and concentrated street activity. Not the whole city; the walkable block radius that locals point to when they say "downtown." Usually has a named identity (the financial district, the city centre, the central district).

§ 3. How to build the list

Search reviews for well-rated restaurants based on local consensus inside the named historic downtown. Local picks matter more than tourist popularity. No cuisine filter — whatever's best by reviews inside the downtown footprint.

§ 4. What ships

  • Minimum 5 restaurants in the downtown, chosen by review consensus on Yelp, Google Reviews, or Local Guide, ordered by editorial standout (best first). Ship fewer only when 5 can't be found.
  • Michelin-starred restaurants ship in their own section, not here.
  • No overlap with Restaurants Near Hotel — these must be different picks.
  • Chains and tourist-trap caricatures are excluded even when geographically downtown.
  • Seafood exclusion: a restaurant whose primary identity is built around fish, shellfish, crustaceans, bacalhau, octopus, or similar seafood is excluded. A general restaurant that also serves fish is not excluded — only venues where seafood is the core concept. Sushi restaurants are permitted; no other seafood-focused venue qualifies.

§ 5. When none qualify

Ship the negative-finding line:

No restaurants in downtown [City].

§ 6. Entry Format(s)

The address should provide the street name and neighborhood. The city is included only when the address is in a city different from the destination [City]. No postal code. The country is included only when the address is in a different country from the destination [City].
