🏨 Hotel Banner

Phase: 2 — Guide structure

Summary: The blue banner at the top of the title page.

§ 1. Entry

[City] [Country]
[Hotel Name] or [Rental Name]
[Hotel Address · Neighborhood] ← per Links.html

The address provides the street name and neighborhood, separated by a middle dot. When the hotel has a published street number, it precedes the street name. No postal code or country.

§ 2. Three-line format

The banner carries exactly three lines in this order: city (uppercase), hotel or rental name, address. No additional lines appear in the banner.

§ 3. Scope

The banner appears on the title page only. It does not repeat, summarise, or link to the accommodation elsewhere in the guide.

§ 4. Hotel naming rule

The hotel or rental name appears exactly once in the guide — in the title banner. Every other reference to the accommodation uses the generic word "hotel" (lowercase). The proper name does not appear in prose, motion banners, day openers, or the day closer (correct: "→ hotel"; never "→ {Hotel Name}").
