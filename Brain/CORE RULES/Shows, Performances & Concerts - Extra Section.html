🎭 Shows, Performances & Concerts

Phase: 5 — Per-section build

Summary: A short list of exceptional shows, performances, and concerts in the destination — opera, classical, theater, residencies, world-class headliners.

§ 1. Reference section

Shows, Performances & Concerts is a reference section, not an itinerary stop. Walk and ride times are measured from the hotel.

§ 2. What ships

Calibration is high — only destination-level performances qualify. If it tours, it likely doesn't qualify.

  • Opera houses — La Scala, Metropolitan Opera, Royal Opera House, Vienna State Opera, Palais Garnier, Teatro La Fenice, Bolshoi, Teatro Colón, Sydney Opera House.
  • Concert halls — Vienna Musikverein, Berlin Philharmonie, Concertgebouw, Carnegie Hall, Boston Symphony Hall.
  • Classical festivals — Salzburg Festival, Bayreuth Festival, Glyndebourne, BBC Proms.
  • Permanent flagship productions — Cirque du Soleil residencies (O, Kà, Mystère, Beatles LOVE), Sphere, long-running Las Vegas headliner residencies.
  • Unique venues & traditions — Arena di Verona, Globe Theatre, Royal Shakespeare Company, Kabuki theater in Tokyo, Beijing Opera, flamenco tablaos in Sevilla and Madrid, Moulin Rouge and Crazy Horse cabarets.
  • Unique festivals & events — Vienna New Year's Concert, Bastille Day fireworks, Venice Carnival, Rio Carnival, Calgary Stampede.

§ 3. Entry Format(s)

The address should provide the street name and neighborhood. The city is included only when the address is in a city different from the destination [City]. No postal code. The country is included only when the address is in a different country from the destination [City].
