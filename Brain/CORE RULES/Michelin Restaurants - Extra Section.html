⭐ Michelin Restaurants

Phase: 5 — Per-section build

Summary: Up to 5 Michelin-starred restaurants closest to the hotel.

§ 1. How to build the list

Source: [Guide Michelin] — stars only (Bib Gourmand and Michelin Plate do not qualify). Search all starred restaurants in [City].

Seafood exclusion: a restaurant whose primary identity is built around fish, shellfish, crustaceans, bacalhau, octopus, or similar seafood is excluded. A general restaurant that also serves fish is not excluded — only venues where seafood is the core concept. Sushi restaurants are permitted; no other seafood-focused venue qualifies.

§ 2. What ships

Maximum 5 entries, top tier first — ⭐⭐⭐, then ⭐⭐, then ⭐. Within each tier, sort by ride time from the hotel, closest first. A restaurant in the [Hotel City] has ride time = 0 and therefore comes first within its tier.

When more than 5 qualify, the last entry is followed by an overflow line:

Not shown: [N] ⭐⭐⭐ · [N] ⭐⭐ · [N] ⭐ more in [City]

§ 3. Entry Format(s)

The address should provide the street name and neighborhood. The city is included only when the address is in a city different from the destination [City]. No postal code. The country is included only when the address is in a different country from the destination [City].

a. When the restaurant is in the [Hotel Building] — always the first entry in the section, before all walk-distance restaurants; no 📍 or motion row.

[⭐ / ⭐⭐ / ⭐⭐⭐] [Restaurant Name] · N.N⭐ · NNN+ reviews ← restaurant's Yelp, Google Reviews, or Local Guide page
↳ [Cuisine Style] ≤80 chars
🏨 In the hotel · [Floor]
🏛 [Hours]
🚫 Closed [Day]

b. All other restaurants

§ 4. When none qualify

Ship a single negative-finding line. The section still ships — the line confirms the search was done.

No Michelin-starred restaurants in [City].
