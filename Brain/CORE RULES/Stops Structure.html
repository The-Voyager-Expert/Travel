Stops Structure

Phase: 2 — Guide structure (stop selection and stop building)

Summary: Complete reference for stops — how to format every stop block, followed by how to research, filter, and select what earns a stop.

§ 1. How to build a stop

§ 1a. Read Brain/Reference/Brain.md (Part 4 — Cities Skip List), find the [City] heading matching the destination, and do not include any of those places before building a stop.

§ 1b. Research what to see in [City], using the following guides as a starting point:

  • Fodor's
  • Culture Trip
  • Rough Guides
  • Atlas Obscura
  • Rick Steves (European destinations)
  • National Geographic Travel
  • Official tourism boards

This list is a starting point — other quality guides are fine.

The test:
Would a reputable travel writer or editor sign their name to it?
Yes → use it.
No → skip it.

Not accepted: random blogs · user-generated lists · affiliate listicles · AI-generated SEO content · content farms — regardless of Google ranking.

Look for:

Historic & medieval: medieval cities and historic centers · cute villages and hilltop towns · historic buildings with rooftop, tower, or viewpoint access (only one-of-a-kind) · ruins and ancient sites · catacombs · local neighborhoods.

Palaces, cathedrals, museums — bar is very high. Only world-defining and visually unlike anything else.

  • Palaces — Versailles level only. Versailles, Schönbrunn, Topkapi, Alhambra, Forbidden City, Winter Palace, Royal Palace of Caserta.
  • Cathedrals — Notre Dame level only. Approved examples: Notre Dame, Sagrada Família, St. Peter's, Hagia Sophia, Duomo di Milano, Cologne Cathedral.
  • Museums — Met / Museo Egizio level only. The Met, Museo Egizio, Louvre, British Museum, Uffizi, Prado, Vatican Museums, Rijksmuseum, Egyptian Museum Cairo.

Castles — soft spot, more forgiving than palaces / cathedrals / museums. Must be intact and visually impressive (not ruins). Approved examples: Neuschwanstein, Chambord, Bran, Hohenzollern.

Modern architecture: striking contemporary buildings · modern observation decks · skywalks · glass structures.

Nature, views, landscapes: panoramic viewpoints · dramatic landscapes (Alps, coastlines, wine hills, lakes) · botanical gardens and parks · hikes up to 2.5 hours · short scenic boat rides (30–60 min) · cave exploration · river/sea cruises when the scenery justifies it.

Rare natural phenomena & expeditions: Northern Lights · volcano hiking · glacier walking · geothermal pools (Blue Lagoon level) · cenotes · stargazing.

Thrills: hot air balloons · paragliding · skydiving · zipline in spectacular settings · glass floors · cliff walks · suspension bridges · SkyJump · helicopter tours · sandboarding · off-road / 4×4 · white-water rafting · canyoning · ice climbing · caving.

Iconic & unusual: gondola rides · sound & light shows on famous monuments · sunrise/sunset in special locations · iconic seasonal events (Bastille Day fireworks, Carnival) · Dead Sea floating · bioluminescent water · overwater bungalows · unusual hotels (ice, underwater, treehouse, cliffside) · famous courts and royal riding schools · Papal Audience in [City].

Theme parks: Disney parks · world-class theme parks with exceptional rides, design, and immersion.

Wildlife & animals: safari · elephant/camel riding · feeding giraffes · swimming with dolphins / whale sharks / rays · snorkeling/diving in truly exceptional locations.

Dining & performances: novelty dining (robot service, underground, in the dark) · shows & performances.

Superlatives: the oldest, longest, tallest, first · historic cafés as regular stops (institutions with real identity) · quirky one-of-a-kind things. Caffè Florian, Caffè Mulassano, Les Deux Magots, Café Central.

The bar:
visually exceptional AND offers a real experience.
Yes → include.
No → skip.

§ 1c. What to exclude

Places & experiences: tourist traps · "nice" or "worth a quick look" (not good enough) · historic sites that are simply old · average or touristy performances · Buddhist temples or monasteries · zoos · cemetery / ghost tours · street-art / graffiti tours · markets of any kind, including iconic ones · shopping arcades whose present function is retail · bookshops of any kind, including iconic literary landmarks.

Grand Bazaar, La Boqueria, Tsukiji Outer, Marrakech souks · Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, Burlington Arcade, GUM, Passage Jouffroy · Shakespeare and Company, Livraria Lello, El Ateneo Grand Splendid, Daunt Books Marylebone.

Tours: private tours · museum-heavy tours · hop-on hop-off buses · Segway · bike tours · e-bike tours · horse carriage · pub crawls · boat parties.

Sports: any kind · sports events (football, F1) · golf · fishing · hunting.

Activities: dolphin watching · city cards / tourist passes · water sports (kayak, paddleboard, jet ski) · water parks · hikes over 2.5 hours.

Tastings & food: wine, olive oil, cheese, truffle tastings · cooking classes · perfume making · pottery · art workshops · truffle hunting.

Lifestyle & leisure: shopping · spa, wellness, massages, saunas · swimming, sunbathing · rooftop bars · nightlife, clubs · casinos · immersive experiences, escape rooms · local festivals, ceremonies.
