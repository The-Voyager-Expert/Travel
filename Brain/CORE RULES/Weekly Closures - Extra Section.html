🗓️ Weekly Closures

Phase: 5 — Per-section build

Summary: City-wide weekly closures by category, so the reader can plan around them.

§ 1. How to build the list

Closure patterns vary by city, state, and country. The list captures any category whose venues share a weekly closure day across the destination.

Single-venue closures that don't match the citywide pattern go on that venue's stop block, not here, and use: 🚫 Closed [Weekday] (see Icon Order and Format.html).

§ 2. Entry Format(s)

🗓️ Weekly Closures
[Category] · Closed [Weekday]
[Category] · Closed [Weekday] & [Weekday]
[Category] · Closed [Weekday] - [Weekday]

§ 3. When the destination has no notable pattern

Ship a single negative-finding line in the section:

No notable city-wide weekly closures in [City].
