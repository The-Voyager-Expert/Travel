🏓 Pickleball

Phase: 5 — Per-section build (California, Arizona, and Oregon only)

Summary: Any pickleball court within 25 min walk of the hotel, ordered by walk time. Mix of outdoor parks and indoor facilities.

§ 1. Reference section

Pickleball is a reference section, not an itinerary stop. Walk and ride times are measured from the hotel.

§ 2. How to build the list

All courts within ~25 min walk, ordered closest first. No cap.

§ 3. Entry Format(s)

The address should provide the street name and neighborhood. The city is included only when the address is in a city different from the destination [City]. No postal code. The country is included only when the address is in a different country from the destination [City].
