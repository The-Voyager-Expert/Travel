☕ Cappuccino

Phase: 5 — Per-section build

Summary: Minimum 5 espresso spots within 25 min walk of the hotel, ordered by walk time.

§ 1. Reference section

Cappuccino is a reference section, not an itinerary stop. Walk and ride times are measured from the hotel.

§ 2. How to build the list

Search for the best espresso on Yelp, Google Reviews, or Local Guides that are about 25 mins walk from the hotel.

Minimum 5 espresso spots, chosen by review consensus on Yelp, Google Reviews, or Local Guide, ordered by walk time (closest first). Ship fewer only when 5 can't be found within the time cap. Time is approximate — going over a few minutes is allowed.
Chain exclusion: American-chain specialty roasters.

§ 3. Entry Format(s)

The address should provide the street name and neighborhood. The city is included only when the address is in a city different from the destination [City]. No postal code. The country is included only when the address is in a different country from the destination [City].

a. When the café is in the [Hotel Building] — always the first entry in the section, before all walk-distance cafés; no 📍 or motion row.

[Café Name] · N.N⭐ · NNN+ reviews ← café's Yelp, Google Reviews, or Local Guide page
🏨 In the hotel · [Floor]
🏛 Daily [H:MMam] - [H:MMpm]
🚫 Closed [Weekday] & [Weekday]

b. All other cafés

[Café Name] · N.N⭐ · NNN+ reviews ← café's Yelp, Google Reviews, or Local Guide page

🚫 Closed [Weekday] & [Weekday]

§ 4. When none qualify

Ship the negative-finding line:

No café within 25 min walk of the hotel.
