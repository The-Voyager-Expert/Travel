🫕 Restaurants Near Hotel

Phase: 5 — Per-section build

Summary: Minimum 5 restaurants within walking distance of the hotel, chosen by review consensus on Yelp, Google Reviews, or Local Guide, for an easy meal after a full day. Distinct from Michelin (destination dining) and Downtown Restaurants (historic core).

§ 1. Reference section

Restaurants Near Hotel is a reference section, not an itinerary stop. Walk and ride times are measured from the hotel.

§ 2. How to find the restaurants

Search reviews for well-rated restaurants based on local consensus. Local picks matter more than tourist popularity. No cuisine filter — whatever's best by reviews and within walking distance.

§ 3. What ships

  • Minimum 5 restaurants within 25 min walk of the hotel, chosen by review consensus on Yelp, Google Reviews, or Local Guide, ordered by walk time (closest first). Ship fewer only when 5 can't be found within the time cap. Time is approximate — going over a few minutes is allowed.
  • Optional hotel restaurant entry — when available. It is additional and does not count toward the minimum 5: the 5 are restaurants within walking distance, while the hotel restaurant is in the hotel (no walk). A guide may ship 5 walk-distance restaurants plus the hotel entry as a 6th.
  • Seafood exclusion: a restaurant whose primary identity is built around fish, shellfish, crustaceans, bacalhau, octopus, or similar seafood is excluded. A general restaurant that also serves fish is not excluded — only venues where seafood is the core concept. Sushi restaurants are permitted; no other seafood-focused venue qualifies.
  • Michelin-starred restaurants ship in their own section, not here.

§ 4. When none qualify

Ship the negative-finding line:

No restaurants within 25 min walk of the hotel.

§ 5. Entry Format(s)

The address should provide the street name and neighborhood. The city is included only when the address is in a city different from the destination [City]. No postal code. The country is included only when the address is in a different country from the destination [City].
