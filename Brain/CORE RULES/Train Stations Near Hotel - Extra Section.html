🚆 Train Stations Near Hotel

Phase: 5 — Per-section build

Summary: The closest station for local/regional trains and the closest station for high-speed trains. The two are usually different stations in Europe.

§ 1. Reference section

Train Stations Near Hotel is a reference section, not an itinerary stop. Walk and ride times are measured from the hotel.

§ 2. What ships

Closest local/regional station + closest high-speed station. Same station for both = one combined entry. No distance limit.

§ 3. Entry Format(s)

The address should provide the street name and neighborhood. The city is included only when the address is in a city different from the destination [City]. No postal code. The country is included only when the address is in a different country from the destination [City].

§ 3a. Local & Regional Stations

The station heading carries the 🚊 icon followed by the station name.
