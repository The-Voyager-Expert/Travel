§ 6. Low-count build comment

When below a section minimum (fewer than 5 entries for Cappuccino, Restaurants Near Hotel, or Downtown; below 5 on any Tours platform; fewer than 2 walking tours), document the reason directly in the guide HTML as a comment on the line immediately after the section header. Without this comment, the guide hard-fails the ship gate. The count itself only warns. Per validate_itinerary.py (2026-05-30):

<!-- low-count: [reason] -->

Example: <!-- low-count: only 1 qualifying café within 25 min walk -->

Count colors: green = exact · amber = maximum · blue = minimum
