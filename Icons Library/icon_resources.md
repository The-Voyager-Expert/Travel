# Travel Icon Resources

## Best bets — colorful 3D / illustrative

1. 🌟 [3dicons.co](http://3dicons.co) — free 3D rendered icons, MIT-style license. Closest to your ChatGPT render aesthetic.
2. 🌟 [iconscout.com](http://iconscout.com) — [3D Travel Icons](https://iconscout.com/3ds/travel) — huge 3D library, mix of free + paid. Great for the look you want.
3. [streamlinehq.com](http://streamlinehq.com) — has a 3D illustrated travel set (free tier).
4. [emojiwrap.com](http://emojiwrap.com) — 3D emoji-style icons.

## 🎨 Free with attribution — colorful illustrated

1. [flaticon.com](http://flaticon.com) — [Travel Pack](https://www.flaticon.com/packs/travel-449) — most variety, free with attribution. Filter by "color" style.
2. [freepik.com](http://freepik.com) — [Travel icons](https://www.freepik.com/icons/travel) — illustrated, free with attribution.
3. [iconfinder.com](http://iconfinder.com) — [Travel](https://www.iconfinder.com/search?q=travel&price=free&style=color) — filter to "color" + "free".

## 🤖 AI-generate (matches your pickleball image perfectly)

1. 🌟 [ChatGPT Image Playground](https://chatgpt.com) — same source as your pickleball render. Prompt: "Generate a set of 15 travel icons in the same 3D rendered style as this pickleball image" and attach the pickleball image.
2. [Ideogram](https://ideogram.ai) — AI image gen, free tier.
3. [Leonardo.ai](http://Leonardo.ai) — AI image gen, free credits daily.

## 🎨 Vector / hand-illustrated styles

1. [iconpacks.net](http://iconpacks.net) — free SVG/PNG, no signup.
2. [svgrepo.com](http://svgrepo.com) — [Travel](https://www.svgrepo.com/vectors/travel/) — what I tried before (Vercel-blocked from my end, but works fine in your browser).
3. [heroicons.com](http://heroicons.com) — clean MIT-licensed icons, more minimal.
